Through regular meetups, stimulating lectures, hands-on workshops, one-on-one mentorship, diverse educational programs, and accessible grants for early stage medical startups, our non-profit aims to be the community and networking hub for young and aspiring medical entrepreneurs.
Enventure is invested in the future of Houston. Help us succeed in our mission to make Houston the top city for life science entrepreneurship.

BioHouston, a 501(c)3 non-profit, shares a common mission with Enventure to develop Houston's medical entrepreneurship community. BioHouston is generously collaborating with Enventure to launch our initial fundraising campaign. Online donations will receive tax-deductible receipts from our online non-profit fundraising platform razoo.com.

Personal checks should be made out to BioHouston with a memo to Enventure and mailed to:

BioHouston

Attention: Enventure Program

6500 Main Street, Suite 1040

Houston, TX 77030
