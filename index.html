houston's medical entrepreneurs

Enventure is building a home for Houston’s medical innovators. The Texas Medical Center’s research institutions and universities educate some of the best and brightest minds in the country. Enventure wants to retain these talented individuals by creating more entrepreneurial jobs right here in Houston. Through regular meetups, stimulating lectures, hands-on workshops, one-on-one mentorship, diverse educational programs, and accessible grants for early stage medical startups, our non-profit aims to be the community and networking hub for young and aspiring medical entrepreneurs.

Enventure is invested in the future of Houston. Through our community, unique resources, and collaborative nature we are confident that we can succeed in our mission to make Houston a top city for life science entrepreneurship.
