The BioVentures Course is a life science entrepreneurship course for all senior graduate students, medical students, post-doctoral fellows and business students that provides students with practical experience in conceptualizing commercial applications for their ideas or existing IP.

Each team will be guided by experienced entrepreneurs for the duration of the course. Students will graduate from this course with practical understanding and experience in how to evaluate medical technology as the basis for starting a new business.

Who: Senior graduate students, medical students, postdoctoral fellows, and MBAs

When: January 9th - March 13th. Classes are on Thursdays, 6 - 9 PM

Where: Bioscience Research Collaborative @ 6500 Main St

This course is available for credit and certificate

enventure is building a home for Houston’s medical innovators.
Through regular meetups, stimulating lectures, hands-on workshops, one-on-one mentorship, diverse educational programs, and accessible funding for early stage medical startups, our non-profit aims to be the community and networking hub for young and aspiring medical entrepreneurs.
