BrainCheck: Mobile Concussion Detection iOS Developer

BrainCheck is a Houston startup that uses interactive tasks on mobile tablets to detect concussion. The company is founded by Baylor College of Medicine neuroscientist David Eagleman.

Position Available

Our software has been developed for Android tablets; we are now going to port it to iOS. To that end, we are looking for an iOS programmer to translate the Android code directly to iOS, or alternatively to Python for use with a cross-platform toolkit such as Kivy. Because we're running in mean and lean start-up mode, programmers willing to work for some combination of equity & a start-up payrate are preferred.

Required Skills

Excellence in programming, 2+ years experience with iOS, attention to detail.

For more information, email sean@eagleman.com

Gene by Gene, Ltd.

Gene by Gene, Ltd., is a cutting-edge genetic testing facility in Houston, TX with an extensive next-generation sequencing laboratory setup. We are looking for highly motivated senior-level scientist, with a strong background in Illumina array and sequencing platforms, to help us develop innovative sequencing-based products.

The successful applicant will mentor and work closely with laboratory personnel to design and troubleshoot sequencing assays. The applicant should have hands-on experience with DNA/RNA extraction and the entire sample preparation pipeline for next-generation sequencing. Some experience with Illumina array technologies is also preferred. Experience with analyzing next-generation sequencing data is a plus but not necessary. Knowledge of human genetics/genomics is required.
