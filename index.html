Space Health Innovation Challenge

The Johnson Space Center has provided challenges to enventure and other partnering innovation groups for a Space Health Innovation Challenge to be held at the BioScience Research Collaborative at Rice University on October 18 - 20.

This is an opportunity for the national health innovation community to join with local Houston innovation groups to provide new perspectives on problems faced by NASA that will keep astronauts healthy while living in space or traveling in the Orion spacecraft during deep space missions.

enventure is building a home for Houston’s medical innovators.
Through regular meetups, stimulating lectures, hands-on workshops, one-on-one mentorship, diverse educational programs, and accessible funding for early stage medical startups, our non-profit aims to be the community and networking hub for young and aspiring medical entrepreneurs.
Join us in giving Houston the momentum it needs to become the #1 city for life science entrepreneurship.
