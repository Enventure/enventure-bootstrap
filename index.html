BrainCheck: Mobile Concussion Detection iOS Developer

BrainCheck is a Houston startup that uses interactive tasks on mobile tablets to detect concussion. The company is founded by Baylor College of Medicine neuroscientist David Eagleman.

Position Available

Our software has been developed for Android tablets; we are now going to port it to iOS. To that end, we are looking for an iOS programmer to translate the Android code directly to iOS, or alternatively to Python for use with a cross-platform toolkit such as Kivy. Because we're running in mean and lean start-up mode, programmers willing to work for some combination of equity & a start-up payrate are preferred.

Required Skills

Excellence in programming, 2+ years experience with iOS, attention to detail.
