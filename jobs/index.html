Gene by Gene, Ltd.

Gene by Gene, Ltd., is a cutting-edge genetic testing facility in Houston, TX with an extensive next-generation sequencing laboratory setup. We are looking for highly motivated senior-level scientist, with a strong background in Illumina array and sequencing platforms, to help us develop innovative sequencing-based products.

The successful applicant will mentor and work closely with laboratory personnel to design and troubleshoot sequencing assays. The applicant should have hands-on experience with DNA/RNA extraction and the entire sample preparation pipeline for next-generation sequencing. Some experience with Illumina array technologies is also preferred. Experience with analyzing next-generation sequencing data is a plus but not necessary. Knowledge of human genetics/genomics is required.
